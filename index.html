About Engrinder

Engrinder gives you the tools to stand out and customize your own grinder.
Using extremely high quality aluminum grinders you achieve the perfect design every time.
Simply email us your own text or image to engrinder@gmail.com, and using our precision laser engraver we engrave your grinder just the way you want.
We will personally inspect each order that comes in to ensure you create the perfect grinder.

Image Spec.

When choosing the image you wish to customise your grinder with a few things should be kept in mind.

Make sure the image is black and white
Keep in mind that everything in black will show up white on the grinder face.
Shaded images should be avoided.
Keep it simple! The best designs are the most simple!

Remember, all orders placed will pass through out control test. If we have any issues at all we will email you immediately.

Feel free to email us, engrinder@gmail.com, at any time during the design stage we are always here to help!
